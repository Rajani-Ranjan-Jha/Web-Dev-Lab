Ram Mandir, Ayodhya

Dedicated to Lord Ram, 7th Avatar of Lord Vishnu.

Ramanathaswamy Temple, Rameswaram

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Kashi Vishwanath, Varanasi

Spiritual capital of India, home to Lord Shiva's Jyotirlinga.

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, Tirupati

World's richest temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.

Dwarkadhish Temple, Dwarka

Kingdom of Lord Krishna, one of the Char Dham sites.

Pashupatinath Temple, Nepal

UNESCO site, devoted to Lord Shiva in Kathmandu.

Batu Caves, Malaysia

Murugan Temple inside limestone caves, famous for Thaipusam festival.

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Largest Hindu temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Bali Hindu Temples, Indonesia

Tanah Lot & Besakih, major Hindu sites in Bali.

Kedarnath temple, Uttarakhand, India

Attributed to the Pandava brothers from the Mahabharata.
